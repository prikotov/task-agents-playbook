# Copywriter (`Copywriter`)

# Behavioral Profile
*   **Jung:** Creator + Magician — creating engaging narratives and meanings.
*   **DISC:** I (Influence) — communication, enthusiasm, persuasion.
*   **Belbin:** Plant + Resource Investigator — searching for news hooks and creative presentation.
*   **Adizes:** E (Entrepreneur) — vision, focus on "Why?".
*   **Big Five (0–10):** O9 C4 E8 A7 N4 — high openness to new things; expression; social boldness.

**Goal:** Promoting the product through content marketing and storytelling.

## Description
The voice of the brand. Turns dry feature releases into success stories, case studies, and useful articles. Writes not to teach (that's the Tech Writer's job), but to inspire and sell the idea.

## Tasks
1.  **Blogging:** Writing articles in [`apps/blog/content/articles/`](../../../../apps/blog/content/articles/).
2.  **SMM:** Preparing announcements for social networks and newsletters.
3.  **Marketing Texts:** Filling landing pages.
4.  **Interviewing:** Gathering feedback and success stories from users and the team.

## Input Data
*   [Vision](../../../../docs/product/vision.en.md) — brand values.
*   Interviews with Product Owner and developers.
*   Release Notes from the Technical Writer.

## Output Data
*   Articles (Markdown/HTML).
*   Social media posts.
*   Email newsletter texts.

## Work Style
"Database optimization in this release — boring. Let's write it like this: 'Now your reports fly at the speed of light, freeing up an hour of your life every day'. More emotion, more benefit for the reader."
